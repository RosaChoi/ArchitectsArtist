Czech Republic | Friendship

Artwork: April 2009

Wasel was among 12 artists who were selected to participate in SMALT ART, an international Art Exhibition in VÍTKOVICE Holding Co., a 180 year-old factory in Ostrava, Czech Republic. The factory closed down its operation for 7 days to allow artists to paint in steel panel with an Enamel media that will then be sent through the factory's own furnaces for 10 minutes at 1562 F°/850 C°. Wasel created 6 artworks 58.27 in x 106.3 in (148cm x 270cm) steel panels and these artworks were exhibited to the public at an Industiral art exhibit. Wasel aslo created his lines throughout his stay. To this day Wasel cherishes his indelible friendship with the Czech Republic's promient artists Aleš Lamr and Václav Malina. Recently Wasel became a friend with Aleš Lamr's son Jan Lamr.

Artist Residencies

  • 2011

  • Dresden, Germany
    Artist Residency Program was organized by German Embassy in Abu Dhabi and Dresden State Art Collections. Wasel created a body of artworks based on his exploration of cultural and artistic journey through out Germany.

  • 2010

  • Pokot, Kenya
    Artist Residency Program was sponsored by UN and UNESCO Ambassador of Kenya, Tegla Loroupe.

  • 2009

  • Ostrava, Czech Republic
    Artist Residency Program was sponsored by Mr. Jan Svĕtlík, President of VÍTKOVICE Holding Co. established in 1892. Produced six art pieces (8'85" x 4'85" | paint on steel panels), using Enamel, fusing powdered glass and dyes to a metal surface by the application of 800 degree Celsius heat.

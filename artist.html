    Artist Residency Program was organized by German Embassy in Abu Dhabi and Dresden State Art Collections. Wasel created a body of artworks based on his exploration of cultural and artistic journey through out Germany.

  • Journey | Kenya
    Artist Residency Program was sponsored by UN and UNESCO Ambassador of Kenya, Tegla Loroupe.

    Artist Residency Program was sponsored by Mr. Jan Svĕtlík, President of VÍTKOVICE Holding Co. established in 1892. Produced six art pieces (), using Enamel, fusing powdered glass and dyes to a metal surface by the application of 800 degree Celsius heat.
